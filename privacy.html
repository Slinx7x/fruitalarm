Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

RoStock is a free fan-made web app that tracks in-game shop stocks for popular Roblox games. We are not affiliated with Roblox Corporation in any way.

What data we collect

  • Wishlist preferences — stored only on your own device using localStorage. We never see or receive this data.
  • Alarm settings — sound type, volume, alarm mode — stored only on your device.
  • Push notification token — if you enable push notifications, your device receives an anonymous FCM token. This token is sent to our backend server solely to deliver stock alerts to you. We do not link this token to any personal identity.
  • Suggestion form — if you submit a suggestion, we receive your message and optionally your email if you provide one. This is used only to respond to your feedback.

What we do NOT collect

  • We do not collect your name, address, or any personal information
  • We do not use tracking cookies
  • We do not sell any data to third parties
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not create user accounts or store passwords

Third party services

  • Firebase (Google) — used to deliver push notifications. Subject to Google's privacy policy.
  • Formspree — used to receive suggestion form submissions. Subject to Formspree's privacy policy.
  • Google AdSense — may display ads on our site. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. You can opt out via Google's ad settings.
  • Discord — our backend reads stock data posted in a Discord channel. No user data is involved in this process.

Cookies

RoStock itself does not use cookies. However third party services like Google AdSense may place cookies on your device for advertising purposes.

Children's privacy

RoStock is designed for general audiences including younger Roblox players. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since all preferences are stored locally on the device, no personal data is transmitted to our servers.

Your rights

Since we store almost nothing server-side, you can clear all your data at any time by clearing your browser's site data for our domain. To remove your push notification subscription, simply clear your browser data or uninstall the app.

Contact

Have questions about this privacy policy? Use our suggestion form to reach us.
