Help & FAQ

Answers to common questions

🔔 Alarm & Notifications
Why isn't the alarm ringing?
Make sure you have added fruits to your wishlist by tapping them. Also check that your phone volume is turned up. The alarm only triggers when a wishlist fruit appears in the stock you selected (Normal, Mirage, or Both).
Will the alarm ring when my phone is locked?
Only if you enable Push Notifications in the Alarm Settings section. On Android Chrome, tap "Enable Push Notifications" and allow the permission. On iPhone, push notifications are not fully supported on web apps — we recommend keeping the app open on screen.
The alarm rang but the fruit was already sold out!
Stock updates can take up to 5 minutes to reach our app after the in-game reset. Rare fruits like Kitsune and Dragon sell out very fast. We recommend being ready before the reset timer hits zero.
Can I choose different alarms for Normal and Mirage?
Not yet — but this is on our roadmap! For now you can set whether to alert on Normal only, Mirage only, or Both using the alarm mode buttons.
📱 Installing the App
How do I install RoStock on my phone?
Open RoStock in Chrome on Android. Tap the 3-dot menu (top right) → "Add to Home Screen" → Install. The app icon appears on your home screen and opens like a real app — no Play Store needed!
Does it work on iPhone?
Yes! Open RoStock in Safari on iPhone → tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen". Note: push notifications don't work on iPhone web apps, so keep the app open on screen for alarms to work.
The app shows a 404 error when I open it!
Uninstall the app from your home screen, clear Chrome's site data for our site, then visit the site fresh and reinstall. This usually fixes it.
🍈 Stock & Game Questions
How often does the stock update?
Normal Stock resets every 4 hours. Mirage Stock resets every 2 hours. RoStock checks for updates every few minutes and shows you the latest stock automatically.
What is Mirage Island?
Mirage Island is a special location in Blox Fruits Third Sea that spawns randomly. It has its own stock that resets every 2 hours and often contains rare fruits not available in the Normal dealer.
The stock showing is wrong / outdated!
Pull down to refresh or tap the Refresh button. If it's still wrong, our data source may be temporarily delayed. Stock data can sometimes lag by up to 15 minutes after a real in-game reset.
Why is my wishlist empty after reopening the app?
Your wishlist is saved on your device. If you cleared your browser data or switched browsers, your wishlist resets. We recommend enabling push notifications so your wishlist syncs with our server.
Will you add more Roblox games?
Yes! Grow a Garden is coming soon. We plan to add more games over time. Have a suggestion? Use our suggestion form!
💰 Pricing & Ads
Is RoStock free?
Yes, completely free. RoStock is supported by ads. We never charge for any features.
Why are there ads?
Ads help us cover server costs and keep RoStock free for everyone. We keep ads minimal and non-intrusive.
