About RoStock

The story behind the app

RoStock was built by a Roblox player who was tired of missing rare fruit stocks in Blox Fruits. We set alarms, refreshed pages manually, and still missed Kitsune every single time. So we built the solution.

What is RoStock?

RoStock is a free real-time stock tracker for Roblox games. When the in-game shop resets, RoStock detects the new stock within minutes and fires a loud alarm — even when your phone is locked — so you never miss a rare drop again.

How it works

1
Real stock data
Our backend reads stock updates from a trusted Discord bot that tracks in-game shop resets in real time.
2
Your wishlist
You pick which fruits you want. RoStock watches only those — no noise, no irrelevant alerts.
3
Instant alarm
The moment your fruit appears in stock, a loud alarm fires. Even on a locked phone with push notifications enabled.

Is it free?

Yes — completely free, forever. RoStock is supported by ads. No subscriptions, no paywalls, no hidden charges.
